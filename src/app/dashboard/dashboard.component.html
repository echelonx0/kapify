The Prosperity Impact Fund (PIF)

The Prosperity Impact Fund (PIF) is a financial support program established to catalyze growth in small and medium enterprises (SMEs) and community-driven projects. The fund aims to create sustainable economic opportunities, promote social development, and drive innovation across key sectors. By supporting high-impact projects, the fund seeks to empower individuals and communities to thrive while ensuring environmental and social sustainability.
