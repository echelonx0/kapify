Frequently Asked Questions

Do credits expire?

No. Your credits remain active indefinitely and can be used whenever you need them.

Can I refund unused credits?

Credits are non-refundable once purchased. However, they never expire, so you can use them at any time.

What happens if I run out of credits?

You can purchase more credits anytime. We'll notify you when your balance is running low.

Are there volume discounts?

Yes! Larger credit packages offer better per-credit rates, with savings up to 30%.

Can I share credits with my team?

Enterprise plans support team credit pools. Contact us for custom team pricing.
